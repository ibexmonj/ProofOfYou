ProofOfYou — Personal API (v0.1)

Thesis: ProofOfYou is a personal API ecosystem. ProofOfYou is data. Structured, verifiable data -- /rules (how you work) and /contributions hosted by you, not a platform. Your work, your context, your domain.

Endpoints

  • /rules — rules of engagement (preferences, values, constraints)
  • /contributions — evidence (commits, tickets, deployments, posts)

Schemas: rules.schema.json · contributions.schema.json

Why

  • Reduce bias: evaluate structured outputs and declared work rules, not pedigree or vibes.
  • Clarity: machine-readable context for people and AI agents.
  • Ownership: host it on your domain. Your data, your control.

Use

curl https://proofofyou.com/rules.json | jq
curl https://proofofyou.com/contributions.json | jq
